Competition Breakdown

Each spring Mozee Motorsports compete against all other SAE Formula Hybrid teams at the Formula Hybrid + Electric competition New Hampshire Motor Speedway. The team competes in events broken up into static and dynamic.

Who we are

Mozee Motorsports is the SAE Formula Hybrid team from the Milwaukee School of engineering. We are a student-led organization that focuses on the design, manufacturing and competing of a hybrid formula style race car. Our team is made up of members from all engineering disciplines working together and gaining skills through hands-on applications of engineering.
