Mozee Motorsports

  • About

    • Who we are

    Mozee Motorsports is the SAE Formula Hybrid team from the Milwaukee School of engineering. We are a student-led organization that focuses on the design, manufacturing and competing of a hybrid formula style race car. Our team is made up of members from all engineering disciplines working together and gaining skills through hands-on applications of engineering. We engage in all aspects of automotive engineering fields, accomplishing MSOEâ€™s goals and furthering the MSOE Mindset. Our members become leaders of character; responsible professionals; passionate learners; and value creators and all of this is furthered through the experiences they have on this team.

    • The Team

    What we do

    Each year we work at a high level to reach our goals of creating a functional and reliable hybrid formula style race car and competing at international competitions. To accomplish this, our team is made up of a series of sub teams which each focus on a major component of the car. The sub teams are split into mechanical and electrical focused with electrical being split into high and low voltage and mechanical split into chassis, controls, powertrain, and integration. With each team furthering their own knowledge and combining that with the others we are able to accomplish a lot and push the goals of students in the automotive engineering industry.
